Here are some helpful videos and tutorials for first-time pokemon users. What if you don’t know how to beat the Elite Four? What if you don’t want to waste time trying to find all the HMs? Make life easy for you.

NEED POKEMON?

  • Catch Mew by typing in the code: 8DEB234A 4C8DC5EC . You will teleport to Faraway Island and you will be able to catch this pokemon. When battling Mew, make sure to use a Dark or Bug type pokemon. Dark is preferable as it is immune to Mew’s psychic attacks. Mew is level 30 when you encounter it, so your pokemon should be approximately lvl 40.
  • Catch Deoxys by typing in the code: 4A99A22B 58284D2D. You will be teleported to Birth Island and you will be able to catch this pokemon. When arriving on Birth Island, follow the triangle. Do not worry about the speed of your character when chasing the triangle, the game only counts the number of steps. Deoxys is level 30 when you encounter it, so your pokemon should be approximately lvl 40. Use either a Dark or Bug type when fighting Deoxys.   • Catch Ho-Oh and Lugia by typing in the code: 842CB8A9 7F8B0149. Remove the code afterward or else you will be stuck in Navel Rock forever! You will be teleported to Navel Rock and you be able to catch these pokemon. Lugia is a psychic and flying type, so it is a wise idea to use a dark type. Bug types are not recommended against this psychic type as Lugia’s secondary type is resistant to bug. If you do not have a dark type, a dragon type is the next best option. Ho-oh is a fire and flying type pokemon, so rock pokemon will dominate it. However, if a rock pokemon is not available, a water type is the next best option. Both of these pokemon are level 70 when you encounter them, so your pokemon should be at least level 75.
  • Need more pokemon? Steal a pokemon from the battle factory! Its simple. First you enter the battle factory and save your progress. Then you pick your three pokemon that you would like to rent. After choosing your rental pokemon, the game will ask you, “Are these 3 pokemon ok?” DO NOT CLICK YES!! Instead type in the code: 734D21ACABA1D48F. This will teleport you to Petalburg city WITH the 3 rental pokemon. In a sense, you got 3 FREE POKEMON!!! And the best part is, you can do it over and over again!

SLOW GAME?

  • Enter this code 958D8046 A7151D70 8BB602F7 8CEB681A and walk into the nearest pokemart to buy Master Balls. The Master Balls are absolutely free.
  • Type in the code B05557898BBD F95340E4DFFD B02FC0E0713 8027CBC08B39 to skip the introduction with Professor Birch. When you start the game and press “Start” the intro will automatically be skipped, and you will start the game in your home in Littleroot city. Key items such as your gender and name will be asked bluntly by the game, but after those are answered, you can start playing the game!
  • Type in the code 10C6FC61E2C9 F64140A276A 510C6FC61E2C9 8F5DDCA742ED 10C6FC61E2C9 79D9F5A5D6FF to receive free Battle Points. Make sure to hold “select” while typing in this code. After this code is inputted, then check your trainer card to see your battle points. Your battle points should be maxed out. Even if you spend your battle points at the battle store, your number of battle points will still be maxed out.
  • Type in the code 49B17DAD4E7F 79C3E2C494FE 7AD8B975DCC0 C04F4AC847FF 70D363C080BF 7AC1B975F6C1 to change your player’s name. As soon as this code is entered, a pop-up window will appear asking for your (new) name. Once you input a different name, the game will continue normally. In some cases, the game may buffer for a while, but after a while, the game will return to normal.
  • Max out your money: to make money fast, type in this code: c051ccf6 975e8da1. Then check your trainer card. You should have maxed out your money. Even if you spend some money at the Pokemart or the Department Store, your money will still be maxed out. This cheat is especially useful before battling the elite 4, because the max amount of money allows use to purchase as many revives and potions as you want.
  • To get more rare candies, type in the code, 82005274 0044, and you will instantly get 99 rare candies. This cheat is especially useful when training weak pokemon. By feeding weak pokemon multiple rare candies, you can increase its level, which in turn increases its stats. It will make the game so much faster! However, the increase in stats will still fall short compared to the increase in stats the pokemon would have gained if it had been hand-trained.
