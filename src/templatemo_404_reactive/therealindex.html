Developers

Hack Boy is a free HTML5 and CSS based web application for gaming of all platforms. Gamers who play GBA, Nintendo DS, PS3, Xbox 360, PC, or other platforms can all utilize this application to their advantage. This site was created at CodeDay Portland at Catlin Gabel School as part of a programming challenge. It is based upon a Bootstrap framework, bringing a dynamic application for users to enjoy. Since CodeDay is themed to be for gaming, our team decided to make an application which directly connects to gaming. Our team consists of Ashwin Bhat, Abhinav Brahmarouthu, Prem Sakala, and Prajwal Sharma.
