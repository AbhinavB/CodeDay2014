Unlimited Sets of Power-ups

To get 99 sets of items, you need to type in the following code: 04C7FE68 00636363 04C7FE6C 63636363. Then, while you are walking on the map, press “1” on the Wii Remote and the power-ups should show up. You will also notice that you have 99 of each of the powerups. Better yet, even if you use a power-up, the powerup count never drops!! This cheat is especially helpful when playing a difficult level that requires a certain power-up. Time: 0:25
